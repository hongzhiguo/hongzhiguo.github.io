Biography

Dr. Hongzhi Guo received his Ph.D degree in electrical engineering from State University of New York at Buffalo under the supervision of Prof. Zhi Sun in 2017. He received his M.S. degree in electrical engineering from Columbia University in 2012.

Currently, Dr. Guo is an Assistant Professor in the Engineering Department at the University of Southern Maine. In the summer of 2016, he was with Genscape Inc. as a research scientist intern.
